About

What is NriDirect?

NriDirect helps NRIs in the UK and Europe complete Indian government document applications without the usual stress of rejected photos, wrong forms or lost VFS appointments. The team handles the full process remotely — you only attend VFS when required, with a fully prepared file. Operating since 2018, with a 98% first-time approval rate across 5,000+ applications. FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to visit an office?
No. The services are designed to be 100% remote. You send documents by email or WhatsApp and only visit VFS or the Indian mission when required.
Can they help if I started an application myself?
In most cases yes — they can review, correct and complete partially filled applications, as long as they haven't been finally submitted.
Is the service charge separate from VFS or embassy fees?
Yes. The service charge covers preparation and support. VFS, embassy or e-Visa fees are set out on the official site or paid directly at submission.
Who does NriDirect serve?
Mainly applicants in the UK and Europe using VFS centres here. For other locations, contact the team to confirm.
Is NriDirect a government body?
No. NriDirect is an independent document assistance service and is not affiliated with the Indian Government, VFS Global, or any government body.
